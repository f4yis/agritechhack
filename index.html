  • Do i need to bring my laptop and other devices?

    Yes, you need to bring your own devices. If, in any exceptional case, there is any hardware or software requirement, the same should be mentioned along with proposal submission, so that it can be arranged, prior to the event.

  • Will accommodation be provided?

    Rooms will be provided for the participants during the event days.

  • Is there any re-imbursement for travel?

    No, we do not offer any reimbursement for travel expenses

  • Will internet connection be provided?

    Yes. It will have standard filters applied

  • Will there be sufficient number of power sockets?

    Power outputs will be available. However, it is recommended that you bring along, the extension cords.
